Payment Agreement

At the start of the development process for your company website or application, you are required to make a payment of 50% of the total development costs upfront. The remaining balance of 50% should be given after launching your site on the web (www).

We will start the development immediately upon receiving of the initial payment.

Mode of Payment: mpesa, cash, paybill no., paypal, bitcoin

Balance Payment has to be cleared / paid before hosting the project.

Upon completion of development & payment, we will setup the hosting, register a domain for you and do the installation.
